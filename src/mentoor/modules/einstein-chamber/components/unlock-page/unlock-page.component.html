Einstein Chamber

What is Einstein Chamber?

Einstein Chamber is a challenging section that measures how you think in your code.

In Einstein Chamber you'll face some very familiar challenges that you crossed by, and other are totally brand new for you.

Here you will start writing what you use but never wrote in your entire career life.

So how it works?

First thing to do is to unlock the chamber.

Once it is unlocked, you can now select what categories from The Shop you want to be challenged in.

Each puzzle has a reward, but be careful, the rewards may backfire on you if you couldn't solve the puzzle therefore you will pay the same amount of gold for the reward.

When your answer is previewed, you can now check the other's answers for the same puzzle and start new puzzle.

Shop
