Writing notes

So, here are some notes to take care of when you write an answer or an article in general.

  1. Capitalize the first letter of every new statement.
  2. Don't forget to use Punctuation in your article, like for example, adding . or ? at the end of the sentence.
  3. When you write a code, put it inside a code block, and define what programming language your code is in if you're writing in Programming Basics track or any general track.
  4. Make your article simple, clear and well-organized.
  5. Put references at the end of your answer for more details.
  6. Test any code you write before posting it.
  7. Double check your answer before submitting it.
  8. Search before answering even if you know the answer, you'll learn new things.
  9. Write with your own words based on your own understanding.
  10. When you read from external references, beside reading and understanding the content of the article, observe how they organize and write down the article to learn from it.
